My time Abroad

Florence, Italy

I had the amazing opportunity to study abroad in this beautiful Italian city and I would do anything to go back! Home to some of the most magnificent authentic Italian restaurants it is definitely a bucketlist destination. It is also home to manu famous Renaissance art pieces and one of the greatest Cathedrals, El Duomo is located in the heart of the city.

Tuscany, Italy

Tuscany: the wine & olive region of Italy! It has beautiful natural lanscapes, and vineyards as far as the eye can see. If you visit this spot I highly recommend a wine tour where they take you to quaint villages and teach you about the history behind the delicious wines you are tasting.

Milan, Italy

Home to another famous Duomo, Milan is a must if you are a fan of fashion and deiscovering the latest trends. Some of the finest shopping, dining, and art in the world is located in this fast-paced metropolitan city.

Budapest, Hungary

Budapest was one of the coolest most unique cities I visited. Home to the famous ruins bars which are definitely a must when you visit. Delicious Mediteranean food, beautiful views,and an overall really fun city.

Vienna, Austria

Vienna is a huge and modern city with tons of beautiful castles, gardens, and architecture. Home to some really cool speakeasies, definitely a place to spend a few days exploring. There is so much to do and see!

Salzburg, Austria

Salzburg is one of the most quaint, beautiful, picturesque little village I have ever been to. Famous for the location of the movie The Sound of Music, I am here to tell you the movie doesn't even do a justice of how amazing this place is! If you visit you must hike up to the top to see the amazing views.

Prague, Czech Republic

Prague is a super cool city. Extremely unique and nothing like I have ever seen before. One of the most famous sites pictured here is the John Lennon wall, but this city has so much more to offer including the famous Prague Castle, amazing Old Town, and fun food stands and markets all around selling cheap and delicious food.

Venice, Italy

Venice: my all-time favorite city in the world. A city of no roads or cars, just canals, boats, and walkways. So unique, amazing, and beautiful. So much artwork, culture, and happiness located in this little city. There is also amazing seafood because it is located right on the Mediteranean ocean.

Rome, Italy

Rome, the capital of Italy and some say the city of love. HUGE city filled with SO much history, artwork, culture, food, wine, and the list goes on. This is a city I definitely need to go back to, there is so much to see and do it is overwhelming. Another amazing Italy city.

Zermatt, Switzerland

Zermatt is a skiers paradise. Home to the famous Matterhorn, and some of the most beautiful views in the world. Enjoy the delicious cheese and chocolate fondue, famous to Switzerland, and end the day with the best Aprés Ski!

Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona is one of the most vibrant, young, and exciting cities.
